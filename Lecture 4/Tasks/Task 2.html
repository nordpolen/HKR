Gaming Overview

Gaming is a form of entertainment that involves interacting with a user interface to generate visual feedback on a two- or three-dimensional video display device.

Popular Game Genres

Action, Adventure, Role-playing, Simulation, Strategy, Sports, and more.

Benefits of Gaming

Improves coordination, problem-solving skills, enhances memory, and provides a fun source of entertainment.

The Future of Gaming

With advancements in technology, gaming will become more immersive with VR, AR, and AI-driven experiences.
